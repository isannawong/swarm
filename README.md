# pond scene

A small interactive scene of a summer pond at dusk. Built entirely from ASCII characters.

---

## the idea

As the weather starts to warm and summer approaches, midges begin to appear hovering in loose clouds above still water, drawn to light, drifting in the breeze. This project tries to capture that feeling. The quiet of a pond in early summer. The slight irritation of walking through a swarm. The way they scatter and regroup like they have somewhere to be.

Everything in the scene the midges, the ducks, the cattails, the column of afternoon light is made from text characters rendered in a monospace font.

---

## the scene

A flat pond sits at the bottom of the frame, marked by a line of tildes. Two cattails grow at the left edge. Three ducks drift slowly across the water, occasionally opening their beaks. A column of pale blue-grey light falls from the upper right, catching the midges in its beam.

---

## the midges

The midges are drawn to warmth and light. Left alone, they cluster together in the sunlit area above the pond, shifting gently as a group drifting, wobbling, never quite still.

**Hover over them to scatter the swarm.** Move your cursor into the cloud and they'll flee outward, fragmenting in all directions. Take your cursor away and they gradually pull back together, finding the light again. The further a midge has strayed, the stronger the pull to return.

Each midge moves independently but the group has a shared centre of gravity that slowly seeks the sunbeam. It's a loose, living thing not a fixed shape.

---

## the ducks

Three ducks cross the pond at different speeds, always facing the direction they're swimming. When they reach the cattails on the left they turn around; when they reach the far right they turn back. Each one chirps on its own schedule a quick flick of the beak, open then closed, at irregular intervals so they never quite sync up.

---

## interaction

| action | what happens |
|---|---|
| hover near midges | the swarm scatters outward |
| move cursor away | midges gradually drift back toward the light |
| resize the window | the scene rescales, everything stays in proportion |

---

## about

Made as a small study in emergent behaviour and ASCII aesthetics trying to see how much life could be suggested through characters and motion alone.

`ascii_pond.html` open in any browser, no setup needed.
